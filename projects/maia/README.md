# Maia Project

## Purpose
Build a working system that helps Maia identify, organise, evaluate, and act on internships or early creative-role opportunities without prematurely filtering them out.

## Maia brief
Maia is currently on a placement year and is looking for internships or early creative roles, mainly in creative direction, exhibitions, and adjacent creative fields. Her main goal is to gain real project experience, expand her network, and develop her design thinking and creative direction skills through strong working environments.

She strongly prefers on-site opportunities, because being physically present matters to her learning, exposure, and relationship-building. She is open to hybrid roles if the opportunity is especially strong, but remote work is not a priority. Her preferred duration is 1–3 months, as shorter placements in different environments may allow her to build experience and connections more quickly. In terms of geography, Europe is the easiest, but location is not a hard constraint; she is open to strong opportunities outside Europe as well. She generally prefers paid roles, but she is also open to exceptional unpaid opportunities if they offer meaningful exposure, strong learning, and valuable connections.

## Hypo mandate
Hypo should not prematurely reject opportunities on Maia's behalf.

The job is to:
- gather potentially relevant opportunities
- organise them clearly
- rank them by likely fit and value
- explain the reasoning behind the ranking
- preserve optionality until Maia reviews the landscape

The goal is not to decide too early, but to help Maia see the landscape clearly and focus her attention intelligently.

## Build order
1. Establish the Maia operating workflow
2. Gather opportunities from web and other sources
3. Rank and categorise them consistently
4. Refine prompts, templates, and tooling based on actual usage

## Outputs this system should eventually support
- opportunity intake and tracking
- ranked opportunity lists
- company / role research notes
- outreach and application drafts
- CV / portfolio tailoring notes
- review checkpoints with Maia and Zad
