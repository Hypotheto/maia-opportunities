# Maia Intake Model

## Intended future workflow
The preferred future intake flow for Maia's project is:

1. Maia forwards a link, screenshot, post, or message to a dedicated WhatsApp account routed to Hypo.
2. Hypo treats that forwarded item as a new intake.
3. Hypo extracts visible data from the source.
4. Hypo researches public details and follows relevant links.
5. Hypo creates or updates the corresponding opportunity report.
6. Hypo adds or updates the opportunity in Maia's board/site.
7. Hypo ranks the opportunity without prematurely dismissing it.

## Principles
- Intake should be friction-light for Maia.
- Forwarding should be enough to trigger the workflow.
- Hypo should organise and assess, not over-filter.
- No external applications or submissions without explicit approval.

## Current status
This is the target operating model.
For now, manual asset drops and ad hoc message sharing are being used to test and refine the workflow before automation.
