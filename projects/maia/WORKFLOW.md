# Maia Workflow

## Core principle

Do not over-filter. Curate intelligently.

## Pipeline

### 1\. Intake

Zad forwards a screenshot, image, link, or post from WhatsApp to Hypo's Telegram account.
Hypo treats every forwarded item as a new opportunity to capture.

Capture these basic facts from the source:

* Title / role
* Organisation
* Link / source
* Location
* On-site / hybrid / remote
* Duration
* Paid / unpaid / unknown
* Domain (creative direction, exhibition, gallery, design studio, brand, culture, etc.)
* Short summary
* Date found
* Status

Intake should be friction-light. Forwarding should be enough to trigger the rest.

### 2\. Research

Before ranking, Hypo researches the opportunity:

* Follow any visible links
* Look up the organisation publicly
* Find the original job post if the source is a screenshot
* Check for application deadlines
* Note team, environment, and credibility signals

Capture what is known, unknown, and inferred. Distinguish live opportunities from historical ones.

### 3\. Triage

Place each opportunity into one of these buckets, with brief reasoning:

* **High-interest** — strong alignment across several important factors
* **Worth reviewing** — plausible fit with some uncertainty or weaker tradeoffs
* **Low-priority** — possible but weak fit versus Maia's goals
* **Hold / unclear** — not enough information yet

Triage is a ranking, not a deletion step. If uncertain, include the opportunity and mark the uncertainty.

For each opportunity, note briefly:

* Why it fits or does not fit Maia's preferences
* What is attractive about it
* What is weak or uncertain
* What would need clarification

### 4\. Record

* Add or update the opportunity in OPPORTUNITIES.md
* For stronger opportunities, create a full report using REPORT\_TEMPLATE.md
* Update the published site in the /site folder

### 5\. Action

Possible actions:

* Ignore for now
* Watch
* Research deeper
* Prepare application
* Contact / outreach
* Ask Maia or Zad a question

No external applications, form submissions, or messages are sent without explicit approval from Zad or Maia.

### 6\. Review

Review the opportunity list periodically with Maia and Zad so ranking logic improves over time.

## Decision rule

If uncertain, include the opportunity and mark the uncertainty. Do not discard it just because it is imperfect.
