# Life Archive

This folder is the structured archive for Zad's life story and possible future book.

## Purpose
Capture raw memories in a form that can later become:
- a memoir
- a founder / business story
- a war-and-reinvention story
- or some hybrid that fits the truth better

## Files
- `timeline.md` — chronological backbone
- `people.md` — important people and short descriptions
- `scenes.md` — vivid moments worth writing fully later
- `open-questions.md` — gaps and prompts for future conversations
- `eras/` — one file per major era

## Working method
When Zad shares an important memory:
1. Save the raw fact pattern in daily memory if needed.
2. Distill durable truths into `MEMORY.md` when appropriate.
3. Update the life archive so the material becomes book-usable, not just remembered.

## Principle
Prefer scenes, turning points, motives, relationships, and consequences over abstract summary.
