# Life Timeline

A working chronological timeline of Zad's life.

## 1992-1997 — Horse years
- Zad finished school in 1992.
- From 1992 to 1997, he was deeply involved with horses.
- Important names from this era: Jean, Kevin, Christina, Matoula.
- Early work sequence to revisit in more detail: Italy first, then Belgium.
- While living and working in a horse stable in Belgium, Zad later picked computers/coding back up.

## 1997 — Return to Greece / USA plan blocked
- Zad returned to Greece to try to get a visa for the USA.
- He wanted to study there and be close to school friends, especially Lambadarios Konstatinos ("Lambas") in Boston.
- The visa was not granted; Zad attributes this in part to being half Greek and half Libyan during a period of poor US-Libya relations.

## 1997-2000 — Libya / business ideas / first money
- Zad traveled to Libya, where his father lived, and got to know both his father and the country better.
- He moved between countries while working on business ideas with Lambas.
- One early business was space imaging: selling high-resolution Earth images for commercial use.
- Zad says this made him his first $200k.
- He describes the period up to 2000 as a relatively carefree life working at a relaxed pace.

## 2000
- Zad met Xenia Vitos.

## 2001 — Razorwax / Norway / Aventurine begins
- In Norway, Kjetil, Erik, Claus Henning, and Ricky had a company called Razorwax.
- Razorwax wanted to create the MMORPG Darkfall.
- Spiros told Zad about the opportunity in 2001.
- Tasos Flabouras was also part of the connection path.
- Zad, Spiros, and Tasos went to Norway and persuaded Razorwax to come to Greece and form Aventurine.
- Aventurine's mission was to create tools and technology that significantly lowered MMORPG production cost.

## Aventurine growth years
- Zad's summary is that Aventurine achieved a major reduction in MMORPG production cost.
- He frames it as roughly $17M versus around $300M for a typical AAA title of the era.
- Many important events from this period still need to be documented.

## 2011 — Major rupture
- Libya's crisis, cancer, and financial issues all hit at once in 2011.
- Zad says he then stopped being close to Aventurine.
- He believes Spiros and Tasos mishandled the company after that, leading to its decline and closure.

## 2013 — Split with Erik and Kjetil
- In 2013, Zad and Erik/Kjetil separated professionally.
- Erik and Kjetil stayed together and formed Hugintech.

## Hugintech / Stasism period
- Hugintech later created Stasism SA.
- Stasism focuses on computer vision technology for children with cerebral palsy.
- Zad stayed close to Kjetil and supported the effort.

## 2023 — Tethys
- Zad joined Tethys Family Office in 2023 after a difficult career transition following the loss of his businesses in Libya.

## 2026 — Stasism funding / DFUW recovery effort
- Stasism is looking for funding and Zad is helping try to raise it.
- Zad has asked that if he helps secure funding, he would like shares and official involvement.
- An American company has approached regarding launching DFUW (Darkfall Unholy Wars), Aventurine's second title.
- Zad made a deal with Erik and Kjetil to give them rights to exploit the game commercially and split the profits.
- He also asked about the possibility of taking shares in Stasism instead of a cash cut.
- Erik and Kjetil came to Greece to search old Aventurine hard drives for missing DFUW build data.
- They identified the relevant server drives, but one drive is missing from the 4-drive REV RAID set: drive #2.
