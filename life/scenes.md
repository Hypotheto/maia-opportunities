# Scenes

Memorable scenes worth writing properly later.

- Getting the opportunity in 2001 and feeling like a dream project had been handed over.
- Going to Norway with Spiros and Tasos to persuade Razorwax to come to Greece.
- The early Aventurine build phase.
- 2011: Libya, cancer, financial issues hitting all at once.
- Erik and Kjetil arriving in Greece in 2026 to search old hard drives.
- Discovering the REV RAID set is incomplete and drive #2 is missing.

## Good scene prompts
- Where exactly were you?
- Who was in the room?
- What did it smell/sound/look like?
- What were you afraid of?
- What did you want that day?
- What changed by the end of the scene?
